# Runbook Template

```markdown
# Runbook: [Scenario]

## Purpose

What situation this runbook handles.

## Signals

- Alerts
- Logs
- Dashboards

## Preconditions

- Required access
- Required tools
- Safety warnings

## Procedure

1. Confirm the issue.
2. Contain or stabilize.
3. Execute the recovery steps.
4. Verify recovery.

## Rollback Or Escalation

- When to stop
- Who to call
- What to communicate

## Evidence

- Where to capture timeline
- Which metrics or screenshots to retain
```
