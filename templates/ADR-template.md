# ADR Template

```markdown
# ADR-XXX: [Decision Title]

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Status:** Proposed / Accepted / Superseded / Deprecated
**Owners:** [Names or roles]

## Context

What problem are we solving? Include constraints, risk, and why the decision matters now.

## Options Considered

### Option A
- Benefits
- Risks
- Operational impact

### Option B
- Benefits
- Risks
- Operational impact

## Decision

What was chosen and why.

## Consequences

### Positive
- Expected benefits

### Negative
- Tradeoffs and follow-on costs

### Follow-Up
- Migration work
- New controls or docs required
- Review date if the decision should be revisited
```
